Why

Sometimes it is hard to decide where to put things. We have an internal wiki which is quick and easy to edit, but I want something that everyone can edit.

Email

Sometimes people want their instances on the Nectar Research cloud to receive email. I believe (and this is why it is unofficial) that all nodes block inbound port 25, and that some of them also block outbound 25, and require you to use an institution's outbound mail server instead. I am not sure why port 25 inbound is blocked.
